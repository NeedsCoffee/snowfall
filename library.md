# Read Robin Bayliss for Free Through Your Library

Did you know you can borrow the Snowball Falls books for free with your library card?

Snowball Falls is also available through your local library, because every good small town should have a cosy book waiting on the shelf.

Many of my books are available through popular library apps, including Libby, OverDrive, Hoopla, cloudLibrary, and BorrowBox. That means you can curl up with a cosy romance in Snowball Falls without spending a cent.

## How to borrow the books

1. **Open your library app**  
   Use Libby, Hoopla, cloudLibrary, BorrowBox, or your local library’s own app.

2. **Search for Robin Bayliss**  
   Type Robin Bayliss into the search bar, or search by the book title.

3. **Borrow instantly**  
   If your library has the title available, simply tap Borrow or Place Hold.

4. **Start reading**  
   The book will be delivered straight to your phone, tablet, or e-reader, depending on the service.

## Can’t find a title?

Please use the Recommend or Request button inside your library app.

Libraries often buy books when readers request them, and every request helps bring Snowball Falls to more readers.

## Available on library services including

- Libby / OverDrive
- Hoopla
- cloudLibrary
- BorrowBox

Thank you for supporting indie books through your local library. Every borrow, hold, and recommendation helps more readers discover Snowball Falls.
